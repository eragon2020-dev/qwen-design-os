# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Authentication
Sign-up and sign-in pages for students and lecturers with role-based access.

### 2. Public Website
Marketing pages displaying courses, lecturers, testimonials, and contact information.

### 3. Student Platform
Dashboard for enrolled courses, video playback, note-taking, and messaging with lecturers.

### 4. Lecturer Platform
Course creation, video link management, enrollment request approval, and student communication.

### 5. Payment & Enrollment
Payment slip upload system with manual verification and enrollment status management.
