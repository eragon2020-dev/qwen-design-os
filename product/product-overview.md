# Study Line

## Description
Study Line is an online learning platform that connects students with lecturers through video-based courses. It provides a simple, accessible way for educators to share content and for learners to enroll, watch, and engage with courses.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Limited Access to Quality Educational Content
Study Line provides a centralized platform where lecturers can easily create and share courses, making educational content more accessible to students.

### Problem 2: Complex Course Management for Educators
The platform simplifies course creation by allowing lecturers to add courses with YouTube video links, manage enrollments, and communicate with students—all in one place.

### Problem 3: Disorganized Learning Experience
Students get a unified dashboard with enrolled courses, integrated note-taking tied to specific videos, and direct messaging with lecturers for a cohesive learning journey.

### Problem 4: Payment Friction in Course Enrollment
Study Line uses a manual slip verification system, allowing users to upload payment slips for enrollment approval—simple and accessible without complex payment integrations.

### Problem 5: Lack of Student-Lecturer Connection
Built-in messaging enables direct communication between students and lecturers, fostering engagement and support throughout the learning process.

## Key Features
- Public website showcasing courses, lecturers, testimonials, and contact information
- Student platform with enrolled courses, video playback, notes, and messaging
- Lecturer platform for course creation, video management, and enrollment approval
- YouTube video embedding (unlisted videos with shareable links)
- Payment slip upload with manual verification workflow
- Integrated note-taking system linked to courses and videos
- User profiles for students and lecturers
- Secure sign-in and sign-up authentication
